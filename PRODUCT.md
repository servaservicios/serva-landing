# SERVA

**Product purpose**: Full-service cleaning and building maintenance company in Monterrey y Área Metropolitana, México.

**Users**: Property owners (residential), business operators (commercial), facilities managers (industrial), building administrators (institutional) in Monterrey, NL, México. They want reliable, professional, locally present providers they can trust and reach instantly via WhatsApp.

**Brand voice**: Precise, confident, approachable, locally proud. Not cold-corporate, not casual-friendly. Dependable like a contractor you've worked with for years and would recommend without hesitation.

**Anti-references**: Leafy "eco cleaning" aesthetics, generic SaaS-cream landing templates, overcrowded icon grids with matching color pills, whiteboard explainer animations, teal-and-white healthcare palettes, hero photos of mops and spray bottles.

**Key differentiators**: 12 specialized services under one provider, Monterrey-native, WhatsApp-first communication, rapid response, residential and commercial scale.

**Colors**: Primary brand green #2ecc70 (oklch 73.5% 0.186 152). Dark backgrounds for hero and trust sections. Clean near-white for content sections.

**Font**: Nunito — use the full weight range (300 to 900). Lean into Black and ExtraBold for headings, Regular for body.

**Primary CTA**: WhatsApp +52 81 1915 8919

**Language**: Spanish (Mexican). Clear, confident. No filler words, no sales-speak, no bureaucratic tone.

**Color strategy**: Committed. The brand green is structural — not a small accent, but a load-bearing visual element in heroes, CTAs, and section punctuation.
